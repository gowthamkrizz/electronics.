Our Story

From a rented unit with two reflow ovens

Stackly opened in a 4,000 sq ft rented unit with two secondhand reflow ovens and a single AOI machine. Our first client was a medical-device startup that needed twenty boards built right, not twenty thousand built fast.

That client is still with us. So is the instinct that shaped the company: take the build that's hard to get right, and get it right. Eighteen years later we run four SMT lines and a full box-build floor, but a process engineer still signs every quote before it leaves the building.
