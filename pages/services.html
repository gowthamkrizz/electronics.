Overview

From a 25-unit prototype run to a 50,000-unit production ramp, the path is the same: design review, sourcing, build, test, ship.

We don't subcontract the steps that matter. Placement, reflow, inspection, and functional test all happen on our floor, which is why a defect found in week 40 can be traced back to a specific reel from week 1.
