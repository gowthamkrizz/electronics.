Overview

A mainboard that has to be right the first time, every time

Veridian Health came to us with a finished schematic for a bedside patient monitor and a hard requirement: zero field failures on the mainboard. We ran DFM review, built a 25-unit pilot batch, then scaled to quarterly production.

Build Gallery

From panel to finished board

The Brief

Challenge & approach

Challenge

The board carried a dense mix of fine-pitch BGA, RF components, and isolated analog front-end circuitry — any solder defect under the BGA package would be invisible to standard optical inspection, and unacceptable in a patient-facing device.

Solution

We added 100% X-ray verification on every BGA joint, built a custom flying-probe test fixture for the analog front end, and ran the pilot batch through full functional test before approving the design for production tooling.
